Crab
with a
KNIFE

A small roguelike where you defend your beloved knife-wielding crab from other bad crabs trying to steal your sandwich. The absolute nerve of them.

Submission for Comfy Jam Summer 2026

Controls

  • WASD: Movement
  • Shift: Sprint
  • Left Click: Attack
  • Left Click (Hold): Heavy Attack
  • Right Click: Target Lock
